What is This?

This is a college radio show hosted by Roommates Claire and Allison. We are apart of Fordham Wavelengths (Fordham LC's radio club). We play music and talk about whatever we feel like. There's a new theme each week! The show is pretty chaotic and silly, so it's a good time for all.

We're live every Wednesday at 10-11AM
